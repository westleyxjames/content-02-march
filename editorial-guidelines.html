Editorial Guidelines

At DailyUpdates, we are committed to producing high-quality, trustworthy content that serves our readers. These editorial guidelines govern how we research, write, and publish all content on our platform.

Accuracy and Fact-Checking

  • All factual claims must be verified through credible sources
  • We cite reputable sources including government agencies, academic institutions, and recognized experts
  • Statistics and data are verified for accuracy and currency
  • Medical, financial, and legal information is reviewed by qualified professionals when appropriate
  • Corrections are made promptly and transparently when errors are identified

Editorial Independence

  • Our editorial content is independent from our business operations
  • Advertisers and partners do not influence editorial decisions
  • Writers and editors choose topics based on reader value, not commercial interests
  • We maintain a clear separation between advertising and editorial content
  • All sponsored content is clearly labeled

Content Standards

We DO:

  • Create original, valuable content that helps readers
  • Use clear, accurate headlines that reflect article content
  • Provide practical, actionable advice
  • Include appropriate disclaimers for health, financial, and legal topics
  • Respect reader privacy and data

We DON'T:

  • Use clickbait headlines or misleading images
  • Make exaggerated or unverifiable claims
  • Promise unrealistic results
  • Plagiarize or use content without proper attribution
  • Publish sensationalized or inflammatory content

Compliance

We adhere to all applicable regulations and industry standards:

  • FTC Guidelines: Full compliance with advertising disclosure requirements
  • Outbrain Safe Content: All content meets Outbrain's content quality standards
  • GDPR & Privacy: Respect for user privacy and data protection
  • Accessibility: Content formatted for readability and accessibility

Corrections and Updates

When we make errors, we correct them promptly and transparently:

  • Significant corrections are noted at the top or bottom of articles
  • Minor corrections (typos, formatting) are made silently
  • Updated information is clearly marked with update dates
  • We welcome reader feedback on accuracy

Reader Feedback

We value reader input and encourage you to:

  • Report factual errors or concerns
  • Suggest topics you'd like us to cover
  • Share your experiences and perspectives
  • Ask questions about our editorial process
